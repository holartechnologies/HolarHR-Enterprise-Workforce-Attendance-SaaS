HolarHR

Enterprise Workforce Attendance SaaS — User Guide

Deployed at: https://holarhrattendance.vercel.app

1. Overview

HolarHR is a multi-tenant workforce attendance and management platform. It serves organizations of all sizes — from small teams to large enterprises — with role-based access control, shift tracking, leave management, attendance reporting, AI-powered insights, and billing integration with Stripe & Paystack.

3. All Features

Multi-Tenant Architecture Each company gets an isolated workspace with its own employees, departments, branches, and data.
Role-Based Access Control Five roles: SUPER_ADMIN, COMPANY_ADMIN, HR_MANAGER, DEPARTMENT_MANAGER, EMPLOYEE. Granular permissions per page and API.
Attendance Tracking Clock in / clock out with late detection, shift-based calculations, early clock-out reason required. Daily unique constraint per employee.
GPS Geofenced Attendance Business+ Browser geolocation on clock-in/out validates the employee is within a configurable grace zone (default 10m, adjustable in Settings). Uses Haversine distance calculation.
Mobile Fingerprint (WebAuthn) Business+ Register your device's fingerprint/face unlock via WebAuthn API and clock in/out with a single tap. Works on any device with biometric sensor — no external hardware needed.
Biometric Devices Business+ Manage physical fingerprint scanners (e.g., ZKTeco). Add devices, enroll employees, import attendance from devices. Plan limits: Business 5, Enterprise 99.
Break Management Track lunch and other breaks during the workday. Duration auto-calculated.
Shift Management Configure morning, evening, or custom shifts with grace periods, working hours, and branch association.
Leave Management Submit leave requests (Sick, Annual, Personal, Maternity, Paternity, Other). Approval workflow by managers. Notifications on submission.
Reporting & Analytics Daily, weekly, monthly summaries. Filter by department or branch. EMPLOYEE role sees own records only.
AI-Powered Insights Enterprise Natural-language HR chat and attendance insight generation via OpenAI.
Departments & Branches Organize your workforce by department (Engineering, HR, Marketing, etc.) and branch (HQ, remote offices). Branches support GPS coordinates for geofencing and are editable/deletable inline (deletion blocked if employees assigned).
Employee Management Full CRUD with plan limit enforcement, search, filtering. Employee creation auto-generates a user account with a temporary password. Assign employees to a Branch and Shift directly from the add/edit form. View device enrollment count per employee.
Branch GPS Coordinates Set latitude/longitude per branch via the inline edit form or "Use My Location" button. Used as the anchor point for GPS geofence validation on clock-in.
Settings — GPS Grace Zone Configure per-tenant GPS grace zone distance (in meters) from the branch's GPS coordinates. Employees must be within this radius to clock in/out. Accessible from the Settings page.
Notifications Real-time (10s polling) notification bell with unread badge. Automatic notifications on leave requests and other events.
Password Management Forgot password flow with reset token. Admin/HR can reset employee passwords from the employee list (temp password shown via toast).
Billing (Stripe) Checkout, webhook, customer portal. Plans synced with Stripe price IDs.
Billing (Paystack) Alternative payment provider for NGN currency. Mock mode available for local development.
Company Settings Company profile, logo upload (stored as base64 in DB — no filesystem), GPS grace zone configuration, plan management.
Profile Page Personal details edit and password change from a dedicated profile page.
Admin Panel SUPER_ADMIN Full system overview: manage tenants (companies), users, view plan distribution and stats.

5. Getting Started — After Sign Up

Follow these steps after registering your company account:

  1. Log In
    Go to https://holarhrattendance.vercel.app/login and sign in with your email and password. Use one of the test accounts above, or register a new company at /register.
  2. Complete Your Company Profile
    Navigate to Settings → update company name, email, phone, address, and GPS grace zone distance. Upload your company logo (PNG/JPG/SVG, max 400KB).
  3. Create Departments
    Go to Departments → click Add Department. Create departments like Engineering, Human Resources, Marketing, Sales, etc.
  4. Create Branches (with GPS)
    Go to Branches → click Add Branch. Add your office locations with GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude). Use the Use My Location button to auto-fill from your current position. Branches can be edited inline or deleted (deletion blocked if employees are assigned).
  5. Define Shifts
    Go to Shifts → click Add Shift. Set shift name, start/end time, grace period (minutes), and optionally assign to a branch. Example: Morning 08:00–17:00, grace 15 min.
  6. Add Employees
    Go to Employees → click Add Employee. Fill in employee details (first name, last name, email, phone, position, salary). Assign them to a Department, Branch, and Shift via dropdowns. View device enrollment count per employee. A user account is automatically created with a temporary password. HR/Admin can reset an employee's password from the row actions menu.
  7. Manage Attendance (with GPS)
    Go to Attendance → click Clock In to start tracking. Your browser will ask for location permission — this validates you are within the GPS grace zone of your assigned branch. Use Clock Out at the end of the day (early clock-out requires a reason). Use Start Break / End Break to track breaks. View attendance status (Present, Late, Absent, Half-day, Leave) in the dashboard.
  8. Use Mobile Fingerprint Clock-In Business+
    Go to Attendance → in the Today's Activity card, tap Register next to "Mobile Fingerprint". Your device will prompt you to scan your fingerprint or face. Once registered, use the Fingerprint Clock-In / Fingerprint Clock-Out button to record attendance with a single biometric tap — no manual clock-in needed. You can register multiple devices (e.g., phone + tablet) by tapping Add Device.
  9. Manage Biometric Devices Business+
    Go to Devices → click Add Device to register a physical fingerprint scanner (name, serial number, IP address, location). From the Employees page, edit an employee and use the Enroll on device dropdown to link them to a device. Attendance can be imported from devices via the API at /api/attendance/device.
  10. Submit & Approve Leave
    Employees: Go to Leave → Request Leave → select type, dates, reason. Managers: Approve or reject pending requests. Notifications are sent automatically.
  11. View Reports
    Go to Reports to see daily, weekly, and monthly attendance summaries. Filter by department or branch (HR and above).
  12. Explore AI Insights Enterprise
    Enterprise plan only. Go to AI Insights to ask natural-language questions about your workforce data or use the HR chat assistant.
  13. Manage Billing
    Go to Billing to view your current plan, upgrade via Stripe or Paystack, or access the customer portal.
  14. Profile & Settings
    Click your avatar (top-right) → Profile to edit personal details or change password. Use Sign Out from the same menu when done.

Note for SUPER_ADMIN: After logging in as admin@holarhr.com, click Admin Panel in the sidebar to access the system-wide admin dashboard where you can manage all companies (Tenants), view all users, and see system statistics.

8. Key Concepts

Multi-Tenant

Each registered company is a separate tenant with its own isolated data. Users, employees, departments, branches, shifts, attendance records, and leave requests are scoped to a single company. A SUPER_ADMIN can oversee all tenants via the Admin Panel.

Employee ↔ User Relationship

When an HR manager creates an Employee record, a corresponding User account is automatically generated with a temporary password. The User enables login; the Employee holds HR data (position, salary, department, etc.). They are linked via employeeId.

Attendance Rules

An employee can have at most one attendance record per day (enforced by unique constraint on employeeId + date). Clocking out early requires a mandatory reason. Late arrival is calculated against the assigned shift's start time plus grace period.

GPS Geofence

When a branch has GPS coordinates set, clock-in/out requests include the employee's browser geolocation. The Haversine distance between the employee's location and the branch's coordinates is calculated. If the distance exceeds the company's GPS Grace Zone (set in Settings, default 10m), the clock-in/out is rejected. This ensures employees are physically present at the office.

Mobile Fingerprint (WebAuthn)

Uses the WebAuthn (Web Authentication) standard to register and verify biometric credentials (fingerprint, Face ID, Windows Hello) directly in the browser. No external hardware, SDKs, or third-party services needed. Credentials are stored per-employee, and each credential has a monotonically increasing counter to detect cloned authenticators. Available on Business+ plans. Employees register from the Attendance page and can add multiple devices.

Biometric Devices (Physical Scanners)

For organizations using physical fingerprint scanners (e.g., ZKTeco), HolarHR provides a Devices management page. Devices are tracked by name, serial number, IP, and location. Employees can be enrolled on multiple devices via the employee edit form. Attendance data can be imported from devices through the /api/attendance/device API endpoint. Plan limits: 5 devices on Business, 99 on Enterprise.

Notifications

Notifications are created server-side (currently on leave request submission). The bell icon in the navbar polls every 10 seconds for new notifications. Unread count shows as a badge. Clicking a notification marks it as read.

Billing Providers

Two payment providers are integrated: Stripe (USD, global) and Paystack (NGN, Nigeria). Paystack has a mock mode for development (PAYSTACK_MOCK=true). Billing is configurable per plan.
